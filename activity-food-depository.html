Packing Food for Chicago Families

Joined a volunteer session at the Greater Chicago Food Depository — sorting, inspecting, and repackaging donated apples in a high-volume, fast-paced team environment, directly supporting household food distribution across Cook County.

Greater Chicago Food Depository · Volunteer Session · November 2025

The Mission
The Greater Chicago Food Depository distributes food through 700+ partner agencies across Cook County. Volunteer sessions support the sorting, packing, and redistribution of donated food to families facing food insecurity.
Impact
Contributed to 4,788 lbs of food packed in a single session — directly supporting 1,512 households across the Chicago area. A high-energy, logistics-driven teamwork experience with measurable community impact.
